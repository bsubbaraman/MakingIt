about me

hi! i'm blair, a second year phd student in HCDE. i'm generally interested in designing tools leveraged towards creative/expressive goals. most recently i have been exploring 3D printing from the creative coding environment p5.js. in the past i studied physics & researched exploding plants, and worked on (technologies for) immersive theater productions.

in this class, i hope to learn, practice & strengthen various design & fabrication skills through projects, specifically to tie making & systems building into research agendas. i have experience with git, analog circuit design, and more limited rhino/grasshopper skills which i can share with others!
